Mayaro Covid-19 Action Task Force - Portfolio Details

For the Mayaro Covid-19 Action Task Force, I designed a user-friendly website that provided up-to-date information and resources for the community. The website included features such as a live news feed, interactive maps, and a directory of local health services to help residents stay informed and safe during the pandemic.
